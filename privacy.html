Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 2025

Andy Arevalo ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy covers all our apps: OnTime Speaker Pro, OnStage Timer Pro, Not Another Twin Calculator, Not Another Stage Timer, Duse ‚Äì Habit Tracker, Student Guardian, and future releases.

1. Information We Do NOT Collect

Our apps do not gather:

  • Personal identifiers (name, email, phone)
  • Geolocation data
  • Contact information
  • Media files
  • Health metrics
  • Usage patterns or analytics
  • Tracking data
  • Device IDs
  • Apple ID-linked information

We do not build user profiles, track behavior, or sell data.

2. Device-Only Storage

Limited information may remain locally on your device:

  • Habit entries (Duse)
  • Calculation records (Twin Calculator)
  • Timer configurations (Stage Timer/Speaker Timer)
  • Meeting Mode information (OnTime Speaker Pro)
  • Email data and settings (Student Guardian)

Data remains on-device unless intentionally exported or shared by you.

3. Payment Processing

All transactions occur through Apple's system using your Apple ID. We receive only anonymized subscription status from Apple, never your payment details.

4. No Third-Party Analytics

Our apps exclude: Google Analytics, Firebase, advertising SDKs, tracking tools, and social plugins.

5. User-Initiated Data Sharing

Exported information (such as timer reports via email/messages) remains outside our control once shared.

6. Child Safety

Our apps contain no child-specific data collection mechanisms.
