1.1 Health, Fitness, and Wellness Disclaimer

IMPORTANT. Several apps in this portfolio handle health-, fitness-, or wellness-related information. This includes CareLedger (medication tracking), PawLedger (pet medication and vaccination records), Healko (Apple Health ↔ Home Assistant bridge), Kickoff (weight and body-fat tracking), and Not Another Fasting Timer (intermittent fasting timer).

These apps are not medical devices. They do not diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any disease, and they must not be used to make medical decisions. The values shown reflect data you have already collected through devices and services you own.

Specifically:

  • CareLedger / PawLedger are personal medication-tracking utilities. They are NOT HIPAA-compliant and should NOT be used by healthcare providers, covered entities, or for any purpose requiring HIPAA compliance.
  • Healko is a home-automation utility that bridges existing Apple Health data to a Home Assistant instance you control. It does not connect to medical hardware.
  • Kickoff tracks weight and body-fat values you log; it does not provide weight-loss medical advice.
  • Not Another Fasting Timer is an informational timer for intermittent-fasting protocols. Multi-day fasts (36 h, 48 h, 72 h) can carry serious health risks and require medical supervision; the app shows an explicit confirmation gate before allowing extended fasts, but this is not a substitute for consulting a qualified healthcare provider.

Always consult a qualified healthcare provider before making medical decisions, beginning a new diet or fasting protocol, or changing medication. Information displayed by these apps (including any AI-generated text) is for informational purposes only and is not a substitute for professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment.
