About

Building apps that respect your time, privacy, and intelligence.

The Story

Hi, I'm Andy. I'm an independent iOS developer who believes apps should be simple tools that help you get things done, not attention-seeking distractions that waste your time.

I started building apps because I couldn't find the tools I needed. Timers that were either too basic or bloated with features I'd never use. Habit trackers that felt more like social media than productivity tools. Calculators buried under ads.

So I built what I wanted to use myself. Clean interfaces. No dark patterns. No data harvesting. Just useful tools that do their job and get out of your way.

Philosophy

Every app I build follows three principles:

⚪

Simple

No feature bloat. Each app does one thing exceptionally well.

🎯

Focused

Designed to help you accomplish your goal, not keep you engaged.

🛠

Useful

Solves real problems. Every feature earns its place.

Privacy First

Your data is yours. My apps don't collect personal information, don't track your behavior, and don't sell anything to advertisers. Most apps work entirely offline.

When an app needs internet access (like for AI features), I'm transparent about exactly what data is sent and why. You can always review the privacy policy for specific details.
