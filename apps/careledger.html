Multiple Patients

Features

📷

Prescription Scanning

Scan prescription labels with your camera to quickly add medications. Uses AI to extract medication details.

🔔

Smart Reminders

Customizable notifications for each dose. Morning, afternoon, evening, or custom times.

Adherence Tracking

See dose history and adherence rates. Know when doses were taken, missed, or skipped.

Inventory Management

Track pill counts and get low-stock alerts. Never run out of medications.

Appointment Calendar

Track doctor appointments and sync with your device calendar.
