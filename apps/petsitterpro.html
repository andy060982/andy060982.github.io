Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about PetSitterPro

How do I add a new client? +
Go to the Clients tab and tap the + button in the top right corner. Fill in the client's details, then add their pets and access codes.
How does GPS walk tracking work? +
During an active visit for a walk-type appointment, tap "Track Walk" to start GPS tracking. Your route will be recorded and displayed on a map. Tap "Stop Tracking" when done. The route and distance are saved with the visit and can be included in your report.
How do I block recurring days off? +
Go to Schedule, then Blocked Time, then Add. Toggle on "Repeat" and select your frequency (Weekly, Daily, etc.). For weekly blocks, select the days you want off. Choose whether it should repeat indefinitely or until a specific date.
How do I create an invoice? +
Go to the Money tab, then Invoices, then tap +. Select a client, add line items for your services, and save. You can then preview and share the PDF invoice with your client.
Can I track my mileage for taxes? +
Yes! Go to Money, then Mileage. You can manually enter trips or use the tracker to record your drive in real-time. The app calculates your IRS deduction automatically at the current rate ($0.67/mile).
Is my data backed up? +
Your data is stored locally on your device and included in your iCloud or iTunes backups if you have those enabled. The app itself does not upload data to cloud servers to protect your privacy.
How do I cancel my subscription? +
Subscriptions are managed through Apple. Go to your iPhone Settings, tap [Your Name], then Subscriptions, then PetSitterPro, then Cancel Subscription.
