Create a Countdown

Creating your first countdown takes just a few seconds.

  1. Open the app and tap the + button in the top right corner.
  2. Enter the event name ‚Äî something like "Sarah's Birthday" or "Vacation to Hawaii".
  3. Pick the date using the date picker. Toggle "Include Time" if you want to count down to a specific hour.
  4. Choose a category (Birthday, Vacation, Wedding, etc.) ‚Äî this sets the default icon and color.
  5. Customize the icon if you want. Tap the icon row to choose from 50+ options.
  6. Set your notifications ‚Äî day of, day before, and/or week before.
  7. Tap "Add" to save your countdown!
Pro Tip:

Add a note to your event to remember important details, like gift ideas or reservation numbers.

Add Widgets

See your countdowns at a glance with Home Screen and Lock Screen widgets.

Home Screen Widgets

  1. Long-press on any empty area of your Home Screen until the apps jiggle.
  2. Tap the + button in the top left corner.
  3. Search for "Not Another Countdown" or scroll to find it.
  4. Choose your size: Small (days only), Medium (days + hours + event info), or Large (full countdown display).
  5. Tap "Add Widget" then position it where you want.
  6. Tap "Done" in the top right to save.

Lock Screen Widgets

  1. Long-press on your Lock Screen to enter customization mode.
  2. Tap "Customize" at the bottom.
  3. Select "Lock Screen".
  4. Tap the widget area above or below the time.
  5. Find "Not Another Countdown" in the widget list.
  6. Choose Circular, Rectangular, or Inline style.
  7. Tap "Done" to save.
Widget Updates:

Widgets show your next upcoming countdown and update automatically. Open the app occasionally to ensure data stays fresh.

Enable Notifications

  1. When creating or editing an event, scroll to the "Notifications" section.
  2. Toggle on the reminders you want: Day of event, Day before, Week before.
  3. Make sure you've allowed notifications when the app first asked, or enable them in Settings ‚Üí Notifications ‚Üí Not Another Countdown.
Notification Timing:

Reminders arrive at 9:00 AM. If your event has a specific time, the "day of" notification arrives at that time.

Recurring Events

Perfect for birthdays and anniversaries that happen every year.

  1. Create a new event or edit an existing one.
  2. In the "Options" section, toggle on "Repeats Annually".
  3. Save the event.

Once enabled, the app automatically calculates the countdown to the next occurrence. When this year's birthday passes, it immediately starts counting down to next year's!

Example:

If Mom's birthday is March 15 and today is March 20, the app shows the countdown to March 15 next year ‚Äî not "5 days ago".
