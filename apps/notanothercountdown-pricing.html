Pricing Questions

Which plan should I choose?

If you only need a few countdowns, Free is perfect. For unlimited countdowns, Lifetime is the best value — pay once, use forever.

Can I try before I buy?

Yes! The free version includes all features with up to 3 countdowns. No credit card required, no trial period — it's free forever.

What's the difference between Lifetime and Annual?

Lifetime is a one-time $4.99 purchase that never expires. Annual is $4.99/year recurring. Lifetime pays for itself after one year.

How do I restore my purchase?

Open the app, go to Settings → Restore Purchases. Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID you used to purchase.

Can I cancel my subscription?

Yes, anytime. Go to iPhone Settings → [Your Name] → Subscriptions → Not Another Countdown → Cancel. You keep access until the period ends.

How do I request a refund?

Refunds are handled by Apple. Visit reportaproblem.apple.com within 14 days of purchase.
