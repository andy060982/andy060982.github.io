What we will never do

❌ Make you create an account

No email signup. No password. No "verify your identity." Open the app, use it.

❌ Store your contacts on our server

Your contact list never touches our infrastructure. iCloud is optional and encrypted by Apple.

❌ Charge per card

Make 1 card or 30. Same price. Same app. The "you have 3 jobs, pay 3x" model is dead.

Free features are free. Paid features live behind explicit user action. Always.
