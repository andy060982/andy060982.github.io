Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Not Another Countdown

General

What is Not Another Countdown?
Not Another Countdown is a beautiful countdown timer app for iPhone. Create countdowns for birthdays, vacations, weddings, graduations, and any event that matters to you. See your countdowns on Home Screen and Lock Screen widgets.
Is the app free?
Yes! You can use Not Another Countdown for free with up to 3 countdowns. This includes all features like widgets, notifications, and recurring events. Upgrade to Premium for unlimited countdowns.
Do I need to create an account?
No account required. Just download the app and start creating countdowns immediately. Your data syncs automatically via iCloud if you're signed in to your Apple ID.
What devices are supported?
Not Another Countdown works on iPhone running iOS 17 or later. iPad support is coming in a future update.

Widgets

How do I add a widget to my Home Screen?
Long-press on your Home Screen, tap the + button in the top corner, search for "Not Another Countdown", then choose your widget size (Small, Medium, or Large) and tap "Add Widget".
How do I add a widget to my Lock Screen?
Long-press on your Lock Screen, tap "Customize", select the Lock Screen, tap the widget area above or below the time, then find "Not Another Countdown" and choose Circular, Rectangular, or Inline style.
Which countdown appears in the widget?
The widget automatically shows your next upcoming countdown. When that event passes, it switches to the next one.
How often does the widget update?
Widgets update approximately every minute. The countdown displayed is always accurate to within a minute or two.
Why isn't my widget showing the right countdown?
Try opening the app once to refresh the data. If the issue persists, remove the widget and add it again. Make sure you have at least one future countdown event.

Notifications & Reminders

What notification options are available?
You can set reminders for: Day of event (morning notification), Day before, and Week before. Enable or disable each option per event.
I'm not receiving notifications. What should I do?
Check that notifications are enabled in Settings → Notifications → Not Another Countdown. Make sure "Allow Notifications" is on and your notification settings (Banners, Sounds, Badges) are configured.
What time do notifications arrive?
Notifications are sent at 9:00 AM on the scheduled day. If you set a specific time for your event, the "day of" notification will arrive at that time.

Recurring Events

How do recurring events work?
When you enable "Repeats Annually" for an event, the countdown automatically calculates to the next occurrence. Perfect for birthdays and anniversaries — set it once and it counts down year after year.
What happens after a recurring event passes?
The countdown immediately starts counting toward next year's date. For example, if your friend's birthday was yesterday, the app now shows the countdown to their next birthday.
Can I see past occurrences?
Recurring events always show the upcoming occurrence. Non-recurring past events appear in the "Past Events" section with a "time since" display.

Premium & Purchases

What's included in Premium?
Premium unlocks unlimited countdowns. All other features (widgets, notifications, recurring events, categories, icons) are available in the free version.
What are my purchase options?
Three options: Lifetime ($4.99 one-time), Annual ($4.99/year), or Monthly ($0.99/month). Lifetime is the best value — pay once, own forever.
How do I restore my purchase?
Open the app, go to Settings (gear icon), scroll down and tap "Restore Purchases". Your purchase will be restored if you're signed in with the same Apple ID.
How do I cancel my subscription?
Open Settings on your iPhone → tap your name → Subscriptions → Not Another Countdown → Cancel Subscription. You'll keep access until the end of your billing period.
Can I get a refund?
Refunds are handled by Apple. Visit reportaproblem.apple.com to request a refund for any App Store purchase.

Data & Privacy

Is my data private?
Yes. Your countdown data stays on your device and in your personal iCloud account. We don't collect, store, or have access to any of your data.
Does the app sync across devices?
Your countdowns sync automatically via iCloud when you're signed in with the same Apple ID on multiple devices.
How do I delete my data?
You can delete individual countdowns by swiping left on them or using the delete button in the detail view. To remove all data, delete the app from your device.

Troubleshooting

The app is crashing. What should I do?
Try force-quitting the app and reopening it. If the issue continues, restart your iPhone. Still having trouble? Make sure you have the latest version from the App Store.
My countdown shows the wrong time remaining.
Check that your event date and time are set correctly. Also verify your iPhone's time zone is correct in Settings → General → Date & Time.
