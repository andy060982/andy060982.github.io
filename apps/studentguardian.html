Frequently Asked Questions

Is my email data secure? ▾
Yes! Your email credentials are stored securely in the iOS Keychain, and your emails are processed locally on your device. We never store your emails on our servers. The only data sent to our servers is the email content for AI summarization, and it's immediately discarded after processing.
Which email providers are supported? ▾
We support Gmail, Outlook/Microsoft 365, iCloud, Yahoo, and AOL. Gmail uses IMAP with app passwords for secure access. Outlook uses OAuth 2.0 for secure authentication.
What are "Trusted Sources"? ▾
Trusted Sources are email addresses or domains that you mark as school-related. Only emails from these sources appear in Student Guardian. For example, you might add "teachers@school.edu" or the entire "@school.edu" domain.
How does AI summarization work? ▾
When you tap "Summarize" on an email, we send the email content to our secure backend which uses GPT-4o-mini to generate a concise summary. The AI extracts key information like dates, events, grades, and action items. Premium users can also bring their own OpenAI API key.
Can I use Student Guardian for multiple children? ▾
Absolutely! You can create unlimited student profiles, each with their own color for easy identification. Associate trusted sources with specific students to keep their communications organized.
What's the difference between Free and Premium? ▾
Free users get unlimited email filtering, calendar integration, and 5 AI summaries per month (English only). Premium unlocks unlimited AI summaries, 15 languages, auto-summarization of new emails, grade/attendance extraction, and the ability to use your own OpenAI API key.
