Coloring tips and troubleshooting

Coloring

How do I fill a region?

+

Pick a color from the palette, tap any region — done. Tap again to change. For shading effects, use Apple Pencil on iPad with pressure sensitivity enabled.

Can I save a page mid-color and come back later?

+

Yes — auto-save every 30 seconds. Closing the app preserves your work. Tap the page in the gallery to resume.

Are new pages added regularly?

+

Monthly content drops. Subscribers get the full library; free tier gets the starter pack + 1 free page from each new drop.

Apple Pencil

Pencil pressure isn't doing anything

+

Settings → Drawing → Pressure Sensitivity → enable. Apple Pencil 1st gen works for tap-fill but lacks pressure for shading.

Hover preview missing on iPad Pro M2+

+

Settings → Drawing → Pencil Hover Preview → enable. Shows where the next color will land before you tap.

Fehlerbehebung

Lost a finished page

+

Pages are stored in the on-device gallery. iOS Backup (iCloud or computer) preserves them. After a restore, your gallery comes back.

Export to PDF is blurry

+

Use Settings → Export → High Resolution PDF (Pro feature). Kostenlos tier exports at 1024x1024 which is fine for screen sharing but soft for print. Pro exports at 4096x4096.

Color picker doesn't show all palettes

+

Custom palettes are tied to Pro. Kostenlos tier shows the 5 starter palettes. Restore Purchase if you've upgraded but don't see them.
