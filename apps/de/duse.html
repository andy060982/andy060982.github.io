Habits, streaks, troubleshooting

Habits & Streaks

How does Duse define a "streak"?

+

A streak counts the number of consecutive days you completed a habit. Skipping a day resets the streak unless that day is a configured rest day. You can also manually mark a day as "skipped" without breaking the streak (Pro feature).

Can I have habits with different schedules?

+

Yes. Each habit has its own schedule: daily, every N days, specific days of the week, weekends only, weekdays only. The streak counter respects the schedule — a Monday-Wednesday-Friday habit completed every M-W-F is a perfect streak.

What's the maximum streak Duse tracks?

+

No upper limit. We've seen users with 1,000+ day streaks. The Best Streak counter never decreases — only the current streak resets on a miss.

Widgets & Notifications

Widget shows old data

+

iOS schedules widget refreshes — typically every 15-60 minutes. Force refresh: long-press the widget → Edit Widget → Done. Or open Duse to trigger an immediate widget reload.

Reminder didn't fire at the scheduled time

+

Check Settings → Notifications → Duse is allowed. iOS Focus mode (Do Not Disturb) suppresses non-Time Sensitive alerts — Duse uses standard notifications. If you've upgraded iOS recently, sometimes the permission resets — re-enable.

Fehlerbehebung

I marked a habit complete but the streak didn't increment

+

Streak increments at midnight (your timezone) once the day's quota is met. Marking a habit complete late in the day is fine. If a streak still isn't incrementing the next morning: force-quit the app and re-open. SwiftData sometimes lags on first read.

Lost my data after deleting + reinstalling

+

Duse stores data on-device only — no cloud backup. Reinstalling clears it. iCloud Drive Backup (set up in iOS Settings → Apple ID → iCloud) backs up app data automatically. After restoring from backup, your habits are back.

Daylight saving time messed up my streak

+

Duse uses your iOS timezone — DST transitions shouldn't affect streaks. If you traveled across timezones, the day boundary may shift. Manually edit the affected entry from the Habit Detail view.
