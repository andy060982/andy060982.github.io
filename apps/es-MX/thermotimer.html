Preguntas Frecuentes

Cooking timers + meat thermometer

Cooking Tips

What temperatures are safe?

+

USDA-recommended internal temperatures are built in: poultry 165°F, ground meat 160°F, beef/lamb steaks 145°F + 3-min rest, pork 145°F + 3-min rest, fish 145°F. ThermoTimer's preset timers count up to those targets.

Does ThermoTimer connect to a Bluetooth probe?

+

Not yet. Current version is a manual entry + reference + cooking timer combo. Bluetooth probe support is roadmapped for a Pro update.

Solución de problemas

Timer alarm didn't fire

+

Check Settings → Notifications → ThermoTimer → Allow. The alarm uses a local notification scheduled for the timer's end. iOS Focus mode may suppress the visible alert but the sound still plays.

Temperature unit toggled to Celsius unexpectedly

+

ThermoTimer follows your iOS region by default. Override per-recipe in the recipe detail view.
