How does Not Another Twin Calculator estimate twin due dates?

+

Twin pregnancies typically deliver 2-3 weeks earlier than singletons. The app applies ACOG (American College of Obstetricians and Gynecologists) guidance: 36-37 weeks for di-di twins, 34-37 weeks for mo-di twins, 32-34 weeks for mo-mo twins.

Can I enter LMP or conception date?

+

Both. LMP (last menstrual period) follows the standard +280 days minus 14 to convert. Conception is +266 days. Twin offset applied after.

Is this medical advice?

+

No. Not Another Twin Calculator is an informational tool only. Always consult your obstetrician for actual delivery planning. Twin pregnancies have many variables this calculator can't predict.

문제 해결

Date picker shows wrong format

+

Date format follows your iOS region. To force MM/dd/yyyy or dd/MM/yyyy, change iOS Settings → General → Language & Region.
