トラブルシューティング & Tips

For complete よくある質問 see よくある質問 page

Widgets

Widget not updating to today's countdown

+

iOS refreshes widgets on a system schedule (every 15 min - 1 hour). Force refresh: long-press widget → Edit Widget → Done. Or open Not Another Countdown to trigger an immediate widget reload.

Lock-screen widget shows yesterday's count

+

Lock screen widgets refresh less aggressively than home-screen widgets. Wake the phone and let iOS refresh, or open the app once.

Notifications

Countdown reminder didn't fire

+

Each countdown has its own notification toggle. Open the countdown → Edit → Reminders → enable. iOS Focus mode may suppress the visible alert.

"Today is the day" notification didn't fire

+

That notification fires at midnight in your iOS timezone. If you crossed timezones the day before, the boundary may shift. Open the app to see the current state.

トラブルシューティング

Countdown shows negative days for a future date

+

Likely a timezone mismatch — the date you saved is interpreted differently by your current device timezone. Edit the countdown and re-pick the date.

Lost data after device restore

+

Not Another Countdown stores on-device. iOS Backup (iCloud or computer) preserves app data. After restore, the countdowns return.
